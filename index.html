I've never done yoga before... What do I need to get started? All you need to bring is your body and some comfortable clothes. We have loaner mats and any other accessories you will need.
Do I have to be flexible or in shape to come? Yoga is for everybody! This class has a focus on accessability which means that we will work together to find a practice that works with your body just as it is.
How much does it cost? Classes are donation based which means you pay what you can/want. If it's your first time just come and check it out and don't worry about a donation. A suggested donation amount is $10-$20.
